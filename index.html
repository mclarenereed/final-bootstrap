About the Brand

Mr. Funk and Mr. Ross say that their aesthetic is a confluence of things that they are fans of: the Grateful Dead, of course, but also punk music and contemporary art. These inspirations give their designs a certain push-and-pull between the childlike and macabre. “We like to make a really cute thing look scary,” Mr. Ross said. “That makes you attracted to it, for some reason, like a positive quote intermixed with a scary image.” New York Times
